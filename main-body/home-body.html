Who Are We?

We are one of the forerunning firms based in Kolkata, providing consultancy services in the field of structural engineering for more than 30 years in and around Kolkata and other parts of the country by providing project evaluations, designs & drawings for construction of various R.C.C. & Steel Structure including supervision of the projects for efficient and successful completion/erection of such structures. Our company is well equipped with Structural Engineers, Supervisors & technicians, Draftsmen, Site Engineers, rendering a satisfactory service to all of our clients. We are one of the few numbers of firms working in this field of Structural Engineering which provides the destructive and non destructive tests of concrete used in any construction entrusted with us, as a part of the entire job. Apart from designing, detailing & execution of the structures, our experience also goes in the field of advance construction technology and improved/alternative construction materials/schemes, conducting various Non destructive/destructive tests in personal laboratory for assessment of quality and desired strength of R.C.C. members, repairing & rehabilitation of various distressed RCC/Masonary structure including heritage buildings.
