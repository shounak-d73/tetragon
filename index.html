Who Are We?

We are one of the forerunning consultancy firms based in Kolkata, providing services in the field of structural and architectural engineering for more than 30 years in and around Kolkata and other parts of the country, by providing project evaluations, designs & drawings for construction of various R.C.C. & Steel Structure including supervision of the projects for efficient and successful completion. Our company is well equipped with Structural Engineers, Architects, Supervisors & technicians, Draftsmen, Site Engineers, rendering satisfactory services to all of our clients, over the years. We are one of the few firms working in this field of Structural Engineering which provides destructive and non-destructive tests of concrete used in any construction work entrusted with us as a part of the entire job. Apart from designing, detailing & execution of the structures, our experience also goes in the field of advance construction technology and improved / alternative construction materials / schemes, conducting various Non destructive / non-destructive tests in personal laboratory for assessment of quality and desired strength of R.C.C. members. We are also one of the few with expertise in repairing & rehabilitation of various distressed RCC / Masonary / Steel structures including heritage building structures.

What Do We Do?

  • Prepare conceptual architectural schemes and master plan for various projects in the form of Residential, Institutional, Commercial, Industrial and Assembly use groups.
  • Provide detailed architectural and structural drawings with landscape details.
  • Provide periodical supervision of construction works to check adherence to drawings and technical specifications.
  • Provide office support to various unforeseen technical problems encountered at site during execution of works.
  • Provide know-how on advance construction technology and adoption of different construction materials and techniques in respect to various conditions of site.
  • In-Situ non-destructive and partially destructive tests.
  • Various R.C.C. structures including Residential, Industrial, Educational, Hospital Building, Guest House Building, Shopping Mall, Auditorium etc.
  • Water shed Management and rain water Harvesting and designing Hydraulic Structures.
  • Provide know-how for repairing and rehabilitation of old & heritage structures.
